Welcome to Halle Fine Art

Your mind is the incubator of your life. Your mind is where the main battle of your life is won or lost. The mind can be a wonderful servant if you give it the right nourishment. The artwork we sell is designed to nourish your mind and help you to build productive mindsets. Have you ever read a book or gone to a conference and a month later struggled to retain the lessons you learned? You are perfectly ok. According to German Psychologist Hermann Ebbinghaus we only retain about 20% of the information we consume, a month later. This means we waste most of our lives relearning unnecessarily.

This problem with retention can be overcome with the use of well comeposed artwork I call wall mentors. Images speak 60.000 times quicker than words. This makes images a far more effective way to convey or retain information.
" I hear and I forget. I see and I remember. I do and I understand" Confucius.

All images in the gallery can be purchased as fine art prints, canvas, acrylic, aluminium and wood.
